About The Game

Developed in just 2 days for ScoreSpace Jam #26, RGB Square is a unique Unity game that ranked 9th for 'Theme' and 14th overall among 147 entries. Released on Sep 8, 2023. I continued to develop this game by adding extra features; in this game, the main stats of our character, such as speed and health, are determined by the ratio of primary colors on the character. We use random colors passing by to adjust our character's stats to our desired balance. Additionally, we can gain abilities based on the color we have on us. There are also black-colored enemies that absorb our colors, introducing a strategic element to managing our character's colors and abilities. The game features a leaderboard system implemented using LootLocker SDK.
