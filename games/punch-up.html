About The Game

An unreleased student project developed collaboratively as a pixel-art fighting and progression experiment. The project explored character combat, rewards, upgrades, and working as part of a small development team. Your fighter earns cash by knocking out enemies, then spends it on upgrading strength, speed and special moves. The game targets mobile devices and has never been publicly released.
