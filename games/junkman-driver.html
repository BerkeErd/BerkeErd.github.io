About The Game

'Junkman Driver' is an engaging arcade game developed with two beginner friends during a game jam. You play as a garbage-collecting car on a mission to clean the streets. Attach wires to collect various objects, sell them to an alien at the garage for nitro upgrades, and outsmart the police chasing you by using your trash tether to knock them off your trail.
