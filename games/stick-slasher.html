About The Game

Stick Slasher is a chaotic physics-based 2D fighting game where every swing can disarm, dismember, or send enemies flying.

Experimental Online Co-op Beta

Steam only. The Steam version includes an optional experimental two-player online co-op beta. It is still being tested, so synchronization problems, connection issues, visual inconsistencies, bugs, and incomplete localization may occur. The original single-player mode remains the primary and most polished way to play Stick Slasher.
