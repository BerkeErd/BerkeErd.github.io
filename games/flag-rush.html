About The Game

Flag Rush is an entertaining marble racing simulation where you can sit back and watch marbles representing world flags compete on dynamic tracks. Aside from enjoying the thrilling races, the game features an in-depth editor allowing you to create your own custom levels and design your very own unique marbles.
