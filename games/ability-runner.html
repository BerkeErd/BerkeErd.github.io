About The Game

Ability Runner - Evolve is an unique 2D Endless Runner style video game with language support for Turkish and English, developed using Unity Engine for Mobile platforms, released on Jan 12, 2022. It is a special game where characters have their own unique passive abilities, and additionally, during the game, you can choose one of the special and different abilities at each level to develop the character and continue running.
