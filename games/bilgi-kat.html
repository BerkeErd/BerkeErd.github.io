About The Game

Bilgi Kat is a trivia game that challenges players across 45 categories with a dynamic question pool. Questions are fetched from a Firebase Firestore database, ensuring a robust and scalable content management system. Players are not only consumers but also contributors; they can submit their own questions and, upon reaching a certain level, gain the privilege to approve new questions submitted by others. This community-driven approach, powered by Firestore, ensures a constant influx of new content, keeping the game engaging. Released on Aug 24, 2023, Bilgi Kat combines knowledge with interactive gameplay to create a continuously evolving trivia adventure. The game contains only Turkish questions.
