About Me

Berke Erdeniz

Game Developer & Founder of Beruke Games

Beruke Games is an independent game studio founded and operated by solo developer Berke Erdeniz. The studio focuses on replayable systems, unusual mechanics, and experimental game ideas across PC, mobile, and web platforms.

Hi, I'm Berke — my passion for game development started at the age of 9, and I've been building interactive experiences ever since. I hold a degree in Software Engineering from Celal Bayar University and work primarily with Unity and C#. Rather than replicating existing trends, my goal is to experiment with fresh mechanics and enjoy the technical challenges as much as the creative process.

Earlier projects span multiple genres and platforms, reflecting a period of experimentation and technical learning before the studio's current focus. Many of them are preserved in the development archive.

Modern projects use AI-assisted workflows for research, iteration, debugging, localization support, testing, and production tooling, while design decisions, integration, quality control, and final implementation remain developer-led.

Outside of game development, I also use generative AI to create music, with several tracks published on platforms like Spotify.

My Vision

To build engaging games that focus on original ideas and enjoyable mechanics, offering players a unique and fun experience.

My Mission

To continually grow as a developer by exploring new technologies, experimenting with creative concepts, and building projects that bring fresh ideas to life.
