About Me

Berke Erdeniz

AI-First Game Developer & Founder of Beruke Games

Hey! I'm Berke Erdeniz, a game developer who's been into making games since I was 9. I'm a one-man team creating fun and creative games under the name Beruke Games.

I got my Software Engineering degree from Celal Bayar University, but I really started learning by figuring out how to develop games on my own with Unity. Now, thanks to my good use of generative AI technology, I feel like I'm even better at what I do.

I'm super passionate about gaming and want to create experiences that really stick with players. Every game is a chance for me to get creative and bring new ideas to life, aiming to go beyond the ordinary and make something special.

My Vision

To create innovative and memorable gaming experiences that push the boundaries of creativity and technology, inspiring players and fellow developers alike.

My Mission

To continually evolve as a game developer, embracing new challenges and technologies, while crafting games that offer players unforgettable adventures and meaningful interactions.
