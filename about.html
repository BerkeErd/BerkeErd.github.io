About Me

As a one-man team, I create creative and entertaining games under the name Beruke Games.

About Me

Hi there, I'm Berke Erdeniz, a dedicated game developer with a degree in Software Engineering from Celal Bayar University. My voyage into the realm of game development commenced at the tender age of 9, with my inaugural projects taking shape in Game Maker Studio.

My Vision

With a zest for the gaming world, my aim is to etch unique and indelible experiences into the hearts of gamers. Every game I craft is a canvas painted with distinctive creativity and innovation, meant to traverse beyond the ordinary and delve into the extraordinary.

My Mission

For me, game development transcends a mere profession; it's a fervent passion. Each new venture is not just a game, but a journey—offering unforgettable escapades to players and an avenue for me to continually hone my craft and evolve in my quest to push the boundaries of gaming.
