About Me

Berke Erdeniz

AI-First Game Developer & Founder of Beruke Games

Greetings, fellow adventurer! I'm Berke Erdeniz, a game developer who unlocked the "Game Creation" skill tree way back when most kids were still learning basic spells. At just 9 years old, I activated my first game development ability, and the rest is epic history!

I graduated with a Software Engineering degree from Celal Bayar University, but my real leveling-up happened while diving into Unity tutorials and experimenting with every crazy idea I could think of. (Spoiler: some ideas worked like magic; others, not so much but that's half the fun, right?)

These days, I'm deeply immersed in generative AI, not just in game development but even in music! I've composed and published AI-driven violin tracks on platforms like Spotify, because blending technology and creativity is what I do best.

My Vision

To create games that are so fun and innovative, you'll be telling your grandkids about them (if you're not too busy still playing).

My Mission

To bring this vision to life by constantly experimenting with new ideas, embracing cutting-edge tools like Unity and generative AI, and pouring my passion into crafting experiences that spark joy, connection, and curiosity.
