About Me

As a one-man team, I create creative and entertaining games under the name Beruke Games.

Who Am I?

Hello, I'm Berke Erdeniz. I graduated with a Software Engineering degree from Celal Bayar University. My journey into game development dates back to when I was 9 years old, starting with my first projects in Game Maker Studio.

My Vision

My goal is to bring unique and unforgettable experiences to the gaming world. Each of my games is filled with unique creativity and innovation.

My Mission

Making games is not just a job for me, it's also a great passion. Each new project is an opportunity to provide unforgettable moments to players and to continually improve myself.
